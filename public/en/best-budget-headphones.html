Best budget headphones in 2026

The best budget headphones are not always the cheapest option. What matters is whether the lower price still gives you enough sound quality, battery life, comfort, and call quality for the money.

What to prioritize

  • Battery life
  • Stable Bluetooth connection
  • Comfort for long sessions
  • Reliable seller and return policy
