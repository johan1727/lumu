Best time to buy a laptop

The best time to buy a laptop depends on urgency, release cycles, and whether current pricing is already competitive enough to justify buying now.

When waiting may help

  • Before major seasonal sales
  • When a new chip generation is close
  • When older inventory is about to clear

When buying now makes sense

If the configuration you need is already priced well, comes from a trusted seller, and solves an immediate need, waiting too long can cost more than it saves.
